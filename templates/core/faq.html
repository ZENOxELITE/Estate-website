Buying Property

Start by determining your budget and getting pre-approved for a mortgage. Then, contact our team to discuss your requirements, preferences, and timeline. We'll guide you through property searches, viewings, negotiations, and all the way to closing.

You'll typically need proof of identity (passport/driver's license), proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns), bank statements, employment verification, credit report, and pre-approval letter from your lender. We'll provide a complete checklist when you start working with us.

The timeline varies, but typically the entire process takes 30-60 days from offer acceptance to closing. Finding the right property can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months depending on your requirements and market conditions.

A home inspection is highly recommended to identify any potential issues with the property before you finalize the purchase. It can save you from costly surprises and provides negotiating leverage if problems are found.

Selling Property

We conduct a comprehensive market analysis comparing your property to recent sales of similar properties in your area. We consider location, size, condition, upgrades, and current market trends to determine the optimal listing price.

Start with decluttering and deep cleaning. Make necessary repairs, fresh paint, and consider minor upgrades. We provide a detailed staging consultation and can recommend professional stagers if needed. First impressions are crucial!

The average time varies by market conditions, location, and price point. Well-priced properties in good condition typically sell within 30-90 days. Our strategic marketing approach helps achieve faster sales at optimal prices.

Costs typically include real estate commission, closing costs, title insurance, property taxes, and any necessary repairs or staging. We provide a detailed breakdown of expected costs upfront so there are no surprises.

Documentation & Legal

Key documents include purchase agreement, title deed, property disclosure forms, home inspection reports, mortgage documents (if applicable), title insurance, and closing documents. We handle all documentation preparation and ensure everything is legally compliant.

Our experienced team carefully reviews all documents for accuracy and completeness. We work with qualified attorneys and title companies, and we maintain detailed checklists to ensure nothing is missed. Every document is verified before signing.

Title insurance protects you from potential ownership disputes, liens, or claims against the property. While not always legally required, it's highly recommended to protect your investment. Most lenders require it for mortgage loans.
